Clinical Trial Triage - Test Inputs Reference

How to use
1) Open /ui/triage.html
2) Select one protocol at a time
3) Fill the values below exactly
4) Click RUN TRIAGE

==================================================
A) ADVERSE EVENT TRIAGE (AE) - Serious/Life-threatening test
==================================================
Protocol: Adverse Event Triage

Patient ID: PX-0201-A
Subject Age: 62
Drug Name / Compound ID: CTT-292
Event Description:
Patient developed acute chest pain and shortness of breath 2 hours after dose.
ECG showed STEMI and elevated troponin. Subject required emergency hospitalization.
Time Since Event: < 24 Hours
Pre-Conditions: Hypertension, Diabetes

Expected pattern in result:
- Severity should trend to LIFE THREATENING (or severe category)
- MedDRA should map to a cardiac term
- Is Serious should be YES
- Reporting timeline should be immediate/expedited

B) PROTOCOL DEVIATION AUDIT (PD) - Major deviation test
==================================================
Protocol: Protocol Deviation Audit

Site ID: SITE-042
Trial Phase: Phase III
Deviation Description:
Eligibility criteria were not met, but subject was enrolled and dosed.
Informed consent version control was incorrect at dosing visit.
Potential data integrity risk identified. FIND-101 and FIND-220 noted.
Subjects Affected: 3
Date of Deviation: 2026-03-25
IRB Notified?: NO

Expected pattern in result:
- Deviation should trend to MAJOR
- CAPA should be required
- Risk score should be high (closer to upper range)
- Flagged findings should include F101/F220 style tags

Indication: Metastatic solid tumor

Dose started on Day 1. By Day 3, patient developed grade 3 rash with fever shortly after dose.
Drug was interrupted, supportive treatment started, symptoms improved over 48 hours.

- Narrative text should be populated with a complete clinical summary
